Delete Your Citify Account

Last updated: May 2026

You can request the deletion of your Citify account and all associated data at any time by following the steps below.

How to Request Deletion

Send an email to smartriga3@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete my Citify account" and include the email address associated with your account.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your profile (name and avatar)
  • Your submitted reports
  • Your comments
  • Your notification history
  • Your account credentials

Retention Period

Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of your request. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

Questions

If you have any questions about account deletion, contact us at smartriga3@gmail.com.
