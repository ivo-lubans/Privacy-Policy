Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Citify ("we", "our", or "us") is a civic reporting app that allows users to report urban issues in Latvia. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: Your email address when you sign in with Google.
  • Location data: Your GPS coordinates when you submit a report, to place it on the map.
  • Photos: Images you upload when creating a report.
  • Report content: Titles, descriptions, and comments you write.

How We Use Your Data

  • To display your reports on the public map
  • To send you notifications about your report status
  • To detect duplicate reports using AI
  • To moderate comments for inappropriate content

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Reports and comments you submit are visible to other users of the app. Your data is stored securely.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Sign-In – for authentication
  • Supabase – for database and file storage
  • Anthropic Claude – for AI-powered report analysis and moderation
  • Expo – for push notifications

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion by contacting us.
